# Insights

1. If you are resilient enough you become your own best mentor. Everybody survives and thrives, be it man or woman in the city or village or the tiger or deer in jungle, they all Survive and thrive because they are resilient enough.

2. Self loathing and impatience is the enemy of strategic and learning mind.

3. Don't let anxiety OR fear of failure OR perfectionism get in way of progress by procrastinating more.

4. Beware of AI going in circles , depending on the constraints u impose and also Ai enabling you or worser giving hallucinations, You will end up going in circles and waste time accidentally.

5. Reliable usage standards:

		- If you require research or compiled list (eg. best free tools) -> Use AI (AI better in compiling and outputting than manual search, especially when hitting in dark).

		- If specific troubleshooting errors and issues -> first search in chrome, then use AI (Occam's razor, AI can misinterpret or overanalyze or hallucinate , while people sharing real world solutions are straightforward).

		- For installations , APP showcase and related tech stuff -> Start with Google : especially you tube video, then only go for AI (Visualization gives clarity and confidence).
